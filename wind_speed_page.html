Wind Speed

Wind Speed does not appear to be affected by how near or far a location is relative to the equator(0 Deg, Latitude). Wind Speed(mph) levels are fairly evenly distributed across the latitude range in our graph. Interestingly, the most frequently found observations of wind speed across all latitude ranges is the 0 to 5(mph) range.
